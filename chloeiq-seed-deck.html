The Solution
Turn every open house into an intelligence event.
Buyers scan a QR code at the door. Chloe does the rest.
1
Chloe Code™ at the door
Buyers scan a physical QR sign. No app download. Instant web experience activates.

60-second quiz profiles the buyer's aesthetic and priorities. 4 DNA types. 94% completion rate.

AI tour guide answers questions room-by-room. Dwell time and room signals captured in real time.

Live buyer scores, heat maps, and AI-drafted personalized follow-ups — ready before the event ends.
5
Seller gets a professional report
Post-event intelligence with buyer DNA mix, demand scores, and room-by-room engagement data.
