How It Works

Five steps. One open house. Infinite intelligence.

A branded QR sign greets buyers. They scan — no app download, no friction. The experience activates instantly.

60 seconds. 4 buyer profiles. Chloe classifies each buyer's aesthetic and purchase priorities with 94% completion rate.
