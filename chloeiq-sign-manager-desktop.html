Texas Statewide — HB 3611 Summary
EFFECTIVE
September 1, 2025 — Amends TX Transportation Code §393.007
WHAT CHANGED
Penalty structure & expanded liability — not the sign rules themselves
FINE STRUCTURE
1st offense: written notice + removal window → up to $1,000
2nd offense: up to $2,500 · 3rd+: up to $5,000 per sign
EXPANDED LIABILITY
Anyone who places, commissions, OR BENEFITS from the sign — agent, broker, and team lead all exposed
SOURCE
TX Legislature 89th Session (2025) · TX REALTORS® Guidance · Texas Transportation Code Ch. 393
