01 — Buyer Experience
4 artifacts

No app. No friction.
Just scan and go.

Buyers scan a Chloe Code QR at the door. In 2 minutes they've built a full buyer profile, unlocked an AI tour guide, and can see every room reimagined in their own aesthetic. Agents know who they are before they leave.

1

Scan Chloe Code → Instant web unlock

No app download. No account required. Zero friction on a buyer's phone at the door.

2

StyleDNA Quiz — 6 questions, 2 minutes

The buyer builds their full aesthetic and lifestyle profile. The agent now knows more than three showings would reveal.

3

Ask Chloe — AI room-by-room tour guide

Buyers ask questions as they walk. Every question is logged. Chloe knows their style DNA.

4

VisionMatch — See the room in their style

Tap any room. See it in Refined Modern, Warm Organic, or Coastal Luxe. Buyers feel seen. Dwell time soars.
